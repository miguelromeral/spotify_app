## 2. PURPOSE OF DATA PROCESSING

The Application requires the User's Spoitfy Account data in order to get acces to the User's favorite tracks, playlists, and other personal info according to [Spotify's API terms of service](https://developer.spotify.com/terms/).

The Application will show to the User the info provided by the API, but only a few part of it will be stored in it's backend, corresponding to the next fields:

- The User's **display name** from their Spotify's account.
- The User's **ID** from their Spotify's account.
- The User's **Track ID** from their own selection, being able to be one of their favorite tracks or any other else in the Spotify's Tracks Catalog.

These fields mentioned above will be visible to every user in the Application once they sign up.
